ABOUT AUTISM COMMUNITY VENTURES

We are experienced educators with a passion for instigating lasting social change.

It is estimated that over 85% of adults with autism are unemployed or underemployed. In the USA alone, it is estimated that at least 50,000 persons with autism leave high school each year. The National Audit Office Report and the National Autism Indicators Report highlight the dearth of adult services in addressing this massive need.

This social problem will only increasingly become a major crisis on a global scale.

As a global community of technologists, educators and parents, we are on a mission to change that.
