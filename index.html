Our Mission

Our mission is to train, inspire and support people with autism as full participants with meaningful employment.

Research

Research has shown that one of the biggest worries for parents of children with autism is focused on the question: “What will happen when my child grows up?”

technological solution

Our technological solution allows us to address this need at a global scale with a replicable model that allows for the customization of content to the needs of specific communities.

Our Goal

Our initial target goal: At least 1 million additional adults with ASD will gain and keep employment.

Autism AllyTM Technology

Present solutions to adult services are vastly limited. Scarcely available services tend to be based on outdated paradigms in job training and disability services that are no longer applicable to the 21st century job market. Our solution is AutismAllyTM – a propriety software platform that addresses the 'skill gap'..
